Population inference and hypothesis testing

2015-07-05 00:00:00 +0200

How do we use statistics to make inferences from a sample to a larger population? How confident are we that these statistics accruately represent these population parameters and how can we use statistics to test whether or not our observations are different from hypothesized values. These are some of the questions that will be addressed in this article.
