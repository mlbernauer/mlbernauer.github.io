Pharmacoeconomics: Sensitivity Analysis

Pharmacoeconomics: Sensitivity Analysis

2015-03-14 00:00:00 +0100

In the example we are interested in computing the incremental cost per life year and the incremental cost per QALY to aid in decision making between two treatment options; Treatment A, and Treatment B. We then conduct a sensitivity analysis to observe how these measures change in response to variations in input parameters; probability of experiencing a particular event and the costs associated with the event.
