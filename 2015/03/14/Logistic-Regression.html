Logistic Regression

2015-03-14 00:00:00 +0100

In this exercise we create a binomial logistic regression model to predict whether someone will default on their credit card based on their current credit card balance and income. Once the model is built, we use it to plot the decision boundary for defaulting. Additionally, we see that the overall accuracy of the model is 90%.
